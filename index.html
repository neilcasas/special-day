Mind Museum
Here's the highlight of the date! Based on my research, the Mind Museum is a perfect combination between science and fun, with all sorts of cool exhibitions that are all interactive. There's also a dinosaur, and this metal ball that can make your hair rise up. Isn't that cool?
Metropolitan Museum of Manila
Here's another museum that we could go to. It's all fancy-shmancy, considering that its totally not copying a certain museum in New York (or maybe its related to the Met, I'm not really judging) But really, it's full of contemporary art created by local artists and the vibes are just immaculate on this one.
